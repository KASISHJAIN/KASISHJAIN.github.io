Background

About me

I'm Kasish Jain, a Computer Engineering student at UT Dallas (Collegium V Honors) from McKinney, TX. I started coding in high school out of curiosity — built games in Java, competed across DFW, ranked top 2 at Digital Divas out of 60+ teams.

At UTD, I stopped just learning and started building things that connect hardware and software. I'm drawn to problems that live at that boundary — where a software bug can physically break something, and reliability isn't optional.

I'm particularly interested in embedded systems, computer vision, and AI safety in physical systems. I believe the next decade of AI will be defined by how systems handle failure — and I want to build those failure-aware systems.

Outside engineering: cricket, photography, baking, and detective games. I speak English, Hindi, Marwari, and conversational Tamil.
